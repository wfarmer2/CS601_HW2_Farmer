Career Background

After completing my undergraduate degrees from Arizona State University (ASU), I started my career in June 2020 for General Motors located in Chandler, Arizona at the company's Arizona IT Innovation Center (AIC) office. I started as a Software Developer in the Customer Care & Aftersales (CCA) organization of the company. Within CCA, the specific space that I operated under is that of the Supply Chain, Warehousing, and Logisitics (SCWL). As a Software Developer I have worked on applications that use neural networks to forecast part demand for the company's supply chain, maintained an exisiting finance application for the CCA Finance team, and have done production support for several global forecasting applications that require on-call duties to remediate production issues. Most recently, I have decided to transition into a Business Analyst role within the SCWL space to better understand the business side of my company's organization to build better systems. Utilizing my technical knowledge and ability to communicate to stakeholders in the company is something that I am excited for in my career journey!

Future Career Goals

As I transition into a Business Analyst role, these are my goals that I hope to achieve.

  • Gain understanding of business processes currently being done in the SCWL space
  • Effectively communicate between all stakeholders to build coherent systems for the organization
  • Personal growth in communication to drive ideas into actionable results and products for the organization
  • Foster an ennvironment that promotes Agile development and thinking
