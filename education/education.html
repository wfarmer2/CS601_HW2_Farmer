Undergraduate Education Background

I attended Arizona State University (ASU) where I earned a B.S. in Business Data Analytics and a B.S. in Marketing in May 2020. While I was attending ASU, I was heavily involved in the Department of Information Systems Club (DISC) that was focused on professional development within the business and technology space, respectively. Through DISC I was able to network with classmates, university alum, company representatives, and even compete in a national data competition where my team placed 2nd in Data Graphics. To learn more about the data competition, please click this page. My time at ASU was full of great memories and learning opportunities that will always make me proud to call myself a Sun Devil!

Graduate Education Background

After starting my career directly after graduating from Arizona State University (ASU), I decided to further enrich my education by enrolling in the M.S. in Software Development program at Boston University (BU). Recently, I have also enrolled in the Certificate in Web Application Development alongside my degree program. The main reason I chose BU for my graduate studies is for the flexible online program that the university offers and the wonderful faculty and staff that help operate the program. Through my learning I have strengthened my current skills, developed new skills, and deepened my knowledge within programming and technology in general. My planned graduation date is May of 2023 and I cannot wait to be able to celebrate my accomplishments in Boston, Massachusetts in the summer!
